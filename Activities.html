About

Who We Are Helping Hand is a nonprofit children's mental health group with seeks to help children with problems specifically, peer pressure. Our goal is to help parents, kids, and teens take charge of their health.

What Do We Do? We aim to give children and parents the tools and confidence to make the best choices in problems concerning peer pressure.

Help Us Spread the Message of Peer Pressure for All Kids We partner with other groups that share our mission to help kids grow up healthy. Contact our Partnership Group to learn more. We hope you find something on the Helping Hand website that inspires you to stay healthy and happy. Please share our content with your friends and family. Thanks for visiting!
